## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser (double-click or drag into browser window)
2. Enter the coefficients a, b, and c
3. Click **Solve** to see the solution and graph
4. Hover your mouse over the graph to see the exact (x, y) coordinates under the cursor


## Types of Solutions

- **Discriminant > 0**: Two distinct real solutions
- **Discriminant = 0**: One repeated real solution
- **Discriminant < 0**: Two complex conjugate solutions
